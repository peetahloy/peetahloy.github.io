Crapaud Smoke Yuh Pipe

Crapaud Smoke Yuh Pipe is a conceptual souvenir design inspired by a popular Trinidadian phrase, blending cultural heritage with modern aesthetics.

The design was conceptualized and rendered using Blender, with final presentation images enhanced and refined in Photoshop and Illustrator.

Objects & Spaces at The University of Texas at Austin | Professor: Kate Catterall

Designed and Developed by Peter Ahloy, 2025
