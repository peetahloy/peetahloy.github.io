WkRm (Workroom)

Year: 2024

Project Overview

Students struggle to find routes and mobility modes that work for them, and are always seeking to optimize their journeys.

There is a need for visual markers and physical affordances that direct, inform, signal, and support mobility on and around campus.

We worked with Austin Transit Department to research, and create solutions to these needs.
